Privacy Policy — Pasalacabra

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Pasalacabra is a daily word-ring trivia game, playable at pasalacabra.com and as an app on Reddit. This policy covers both. We do not require an account, email address, or payment to play either version, and we do not sell your data.

What we collect — Reddit app

  • Your Reddit user ID and username, provided to us by Reddit's developer platform (Devvit) when you open the app, so we can track your own progress and show leaderboards.
  • Game results: which letters you answered correctly, your time used, and your win streak — stored so the daily leaderboard and streak features work.
  • Shared results: if you tap "share," we post a comment on your behalf (using Reddit's own API, and only when you take that action) containing an image of your results card. We never post on your behalf without you explicitly choosing to share.

This data is stored in our application database with an automatic expiry of 30–90 days depending on the record, after which it is deleted. The daily puzzle content itself is fetched from a private storage bucket we control and contains no personal data.

What we collect — Website (pasalacabra.com)

  • Anonymous analytics via Pendo: a randomly generated ID stored in your browser's local storage, used to understand feature usage. This ID is not tied to your name, email, or any Reddit account.
  • Voice input (optional): if you use voice-answer mode, your spoken audio is sent to Microsoft Azure Cognitive Services for speech-to-text conversion so your answer can be recognized. We do not store recordings of your voice ourselves; that processing is governed by Microsoft's own privacy terms. If you don't use voice mode, no audio is captured.

Third parties involved

  • Reddit, Inc. — operates the platform the Reddit app runs on; your use of Reddit itself is separately governed by Reddit's Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Azure — speech-to-text processing (website voice mode only).
  • Pendo — anonymous product analytics (website only).
  • Amazon Web Services (S3) — hosts the daily puzzle content file the app fetches. No personal data is stored there.

Data retention and your choices

Reddit-app game/leaderboard records expire automatically after 30–90 days. Clearing your browser's site data removes the anonymous website analytics ID. To stop the Reddit app from accessing your Reddit account, remove/uninstall it from the subreddit or via your Reddit apps settings.

Children's privacy

Pasalacabra is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them beyond what Reddit's own platform-wide age requirements already govern for the Reddit app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the game changes. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact
